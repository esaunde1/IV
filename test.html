Once you decide where you would like to Visit it is time to book for visit. In order to do this you must know a few details such as the date and time you plan on visiting. The most important aspect of booking your Visit is the IV Survey. We want to match you up with an Insider who is similar to you. In order for us to do that, you must tell us a little bit about yourself.

Pick a College

The only thing left to do is enjoy your visit! Feel comfortable asking your Insider anything and everything. They are there to answer YOUR questions. We suggest going WITHOUT your parents... who needs them, anyway? Make sure to write down as many questions about the school as you can think of, prior to the Visit.

If you feel your Insider did a great job... TIP THEM! you will be in college soon and will understand how far a few dollars can go.
