At InsiderVisit, Students Come First

We are in no way affiliated with any college or university! Get the REAL view of the school, not just what they WANT you to see.

InsiderVisit helps high school students decide if a college would be a good match for them. Our Insiders will be your friend on campus who will divulge information that may not be as openly talked about on the school provided tours, such as the truth about nightlife, the vibe or cliques on campus, the plusses and minuses of the dining options, a full explanation of the differences of the living communities, and anything else you desire to know about

InsiderVisit provides you with a student at the college you are visiting, known as an Insider. Your Insider will meet with you one on one to help you better understand that college. Your Insider will be personally matched up to you based on your interests, and will act like a friend sharing their experiences. Your friend will share their objective experience, not to convince you, but to enlighten you about the college. Unlike school tours, each opportunity with InsiderVisit is unique for the prospective student. This is the perfect forum to have any questions answered, free of judgment. InsiderVisit is designed for students. We recommend that parents don't go on the Visit, but that they ask questions at the beginning or end.
